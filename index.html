Travel the world without leaving your seat

With Heli Bhumi for Chrome, fly anywhere in seconds and explore hundreds of 3D cities right in your browser. Roll the dice to discover someplace new, take a guided tour with Voyager, and create your own maps and stories. Coming soon to more browsers.

The whole world, in your hands

Heli Bhumi for mobile enables you to browse the globe with a swipe of your finger on your phone or tablet.

Create maps with advanced tools

Heli Bhumi Pro on desktop is available for users with advanced feature needs. Import and export GIS data, and go back in time with historical imagery. Available on PC, Mac, or Linux.
